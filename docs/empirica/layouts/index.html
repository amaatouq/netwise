Be Productive

Empirical will handle all the tedious logistics of running experiments from crossing your independent variables to form treatments, randomization, synchronization, waiting rooms, storing data, and managing players. You get straight to what is really unique about your research and what interests you, whatever that may be.

Be Expansive & Realistic

Empirical allows you to study complex tasks and setup interactions that happen over any period, from seconds to months, or among any number of people, from a single player to groups of any size. Don’t be constrained by standard behavioral experiments.

Simple or Complex

It is easy to implement simple A/B tests with independent players in Empirica. But it's as easy to implement group experiments with real-time synchronous or asynchronous interactions in a factorial or within-subjects designs, or more complex designs involving multiple types of units (e.g., users, content, roles), and conditional logic.

Real or Artificial

Empirica provides you with easily configurable artificial players that can be included in the experimental game. This allows for studying Human + AI social systems. This hybrid system could be the future of our society!

Flexible Standards

Empirica is based on the widely-used web technology standards: Javascript and React.js. This means you can create your own experimental games with only little prior programming knowledge.

Don’t miss the action

Deploy your experiment from a live web interface and watch the progress in real-time with the ability to create one-way mirrors to observe the behavior of players in your virtual lab.
